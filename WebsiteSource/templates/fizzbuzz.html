fizzbuzz

Welcome to fizzbuzz! The rules are simple, type in the box below the number one and start your counting journey. The computer will play along with you! If your number is divisible by 3 you type fizz, if divisible by 5 you type buzz, and fizzbuzz if its divisible by 15. Reach fizzbuzz for a spinning surprise! The first box is your input. The second is the computer's and the last your results. If you get something wrong, don't worry it automatically resets! Just type 1 in the first box and Enjoy! Please press the button at the bottom to submit your answers. Thank you! (example: 1, 2, fizz, 4, buzz and so on)
